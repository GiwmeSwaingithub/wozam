📋 Privacy  ·  Wozam

motivation app · zero data last update: 4 march 2026

This privacy policy explains how sheber (“we”, “us”, “developer”) handles your information when you use the Wozam mobile application. We believe in complete transparency: 🔒 the app collects nothing, requires no permissions, and works entirely offline.

🚫 Absolutely no data collection

Wozam does not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal information or usage data. No sign‑up, no login, no identifiers, no location, no cookies, no analytics — nothing. You are anonymous when using the app.

🔍 Information we do NOT collect

no name / email no device ID no location no contacts no photos no usage stats no crash logs

Because the app is built purely for motivation (quotes, affirmations, minimal interface), it does not require any internet access, storage permission, or special access. We deliberately avoid including any third‑party SDKs (advertising, analytics, crash reporters) that could collect data.

📱 Permissions & app access

Zero permissions requested. Wozam does not ask for access to your camera, microphone, contacts, calendar, files, or any other sensitive or non‑sensitive capability. It runs in isolation and does not interact with other apps or system services. You can use it entirely offline — no Wi‑Fi or mobile data needed.

🧒 Children's privacy

Our app is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. Since we collect no personal information whatsoever, there is no risk of inadvertent data collection from minors. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) by design — we simply have no data to collect.

🤝 Sharing of information

We do not share any information because we don't possess any. No data is sold, traded, or transferred to third parties. There are no third‑party services embedded (no ads, no analytics, no crash reporting tools).

🛡️ Data security

As no user data exists within the app, there are no databases, servers, or logs that could be compromised. The app functions completely offline, eliminating any data transmission risks. Your privacy is absolute by design.

🔗 Changes to this policy

If this privacy policy ever needs to be updated (for example, to reflect new app functionality), the revised version will be posted here with an updated “last update” date. We will never retroactively change how we handle your data without clear notice. Continued use of Wozam after changes implies acceptance of the updated policy.
